# BetterTouchTool: Normal Mice

BetterTouchTool can make your mouse super powerful. For example you can assign actions to any of the mouse's buttons, use mouse gestures, configure short & long click actions. You can activate smooth scrolling or simulate various macOS trackpad gestures. Have a look at the following sections to see how:

* [Scroll Modifiers](4001_scroll_modifiers.md)
* [Configure Logitech Mice](4000_logitech.md)
* [Help: My mouse's buttons are not recognized by BTT](803_normal_mice_problems.md)
